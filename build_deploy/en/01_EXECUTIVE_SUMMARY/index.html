⚡ THE PITCH​

Read this once. Something will shift.

Imagine your money growing faster every time you sleep well. Every time you manage stress effectively — you earn more. Every time your body is in balance — the protocol rewards you in USDC.

The Problem: A World Split in Two​

Seven billion people work on their health — spending time, money, willpower. 73% abandon their fitness goals within three months. The reason is simple: no immediate reward.

Meanwhile, $195 billion sits locked in DeFi protocols — invisible, complex, inaccessible to ordinary people. 24-word seed phrases. Gas fees. Bridges. Audits. Liquidations.

These two worlds have never intersected. Until Wellex.

The Solution: Emotional Mining Protocol​

In Bitcoin Mining, computing power converts into cryptocurrency. In Emotional Mining, your physical and emotional wellbeing converts into real DeFi yield.

Wellex Band → Biometrics → WVI (0–100) → Target Yield 0–20%/month

WVI (Wellex Vitality Index) is your "hash rate." A daily score from 0 to 100, calculated from three sources:

  • Emotional State (35%) — 11 core emotions via HRV and skin conductance, LSTM neural network with 80–95% accuracy
  • Recovery (35%) — sleep quality, phases, recovery score
  • Physical Activity (30%) — not just steps, but intensity and consistency

The higher your WVI, the higher your target yield. WVI 90+ delivers up to 20%/month from real DeFi — not token emission. From eight independent sources: lending, RWA, arbitrage, basis trading.

⚠️ Target Yield is not a guarantee. Actual yield depends on DeFi market conditions and Dynamic Yield Cap. Historical average blended: ~1.5–3.5%/month.

Why Now (2026)​

Five forces have converged at a single point — for the first time in history:

1. DeFi has matured. Ethena Protocol — $5B+ TVL on funding rate arbitrage. Aave v3 — $20B+ TVL. Protocols have proven resilience. The yield is real.

2. AI coaching works. GPT-4o, on-device inference, emotion detection accuracy 80–95% — this isn't a lab experiment. This is a 2026 product.

3. Account Abstraction (ERC-4337) removed barriers. No seed phrase needed. Gas is invisible (Wellex Paymaster). DeFi is now as simple as a banking app.

4. Move-to-Earn left a vacuum. STEPN fell 97% from ATH. Sweatcoin — 90%. The market knows: token emission doesn't work. The space for a real yield protocol is wide open.

5. Biometrics have become precise. TEE chips (Infineon OPTIGA Trust M) make biometric verification hardware-secured. Gaming the system is impossible — this isn't a step counter.

The Product: Three Layers, One Ecosystem​

Wellex Band — a smart bracelet with six sensors: PPG, ECG, SpO2, EDA, accelerometer, thermometer. 14-day battery. Built-in TEE chip for cryptographic biometric signing. Free with $19/month subscription.

APP (iOS/Android) — a clean wellness world. WVI in real time, AI Coach (text, audio, video), community for bracelet owners (WHOOP model: groups, challenges, leaderboards). No dollar amounts — only % yield. Because health isn't about money.

WEB Platform — full financial control. WellexVault (ERC-4626) on 7 EVM chains plus Tron and Solana. Card on-ramp (Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, SEPA, PIX). MLM dashboard. Automated management via AI Wallet Agent. All of this — with zero technical knowledge required.

Five Competitive Moats That Cannot Be Copied​

1. WVI Algorithm (patent pending). 18–24 months to replicate — even with unlimited budget.

2. Biometric dataset. Every day of user activity adds to the training data. By 10,000 users, the dataset becomes an insurmountable competitive advantage.

3. TEE Anti-Cheat hardware. WVI cannot be faked from a phone — only from a genuine bracelet with a TEE chip.

4. "Emotional Mining" — registered trademark. The category has been named. Whoever names a category owns it.

5. Partner network with payment history. After 12 months of operation, the network becomes self-reproducing. Switching costs grow exponentially.

02 · PRODUCT VISION

Version: 5.0 | Date: 05.03.2026 | Status: Public

Complete vision of the Wellex platform: Emotional Mining, dual APP/WEB architecture, Multi-EVM, Card On-Ramp, WHOOP-model community, 3-year strategy

Mission​

Wellex creates the Emotional Mining industry — a process where caring for health and emotional wellbeing is converted into a financial asset. We are building the world's first ecosystem that unites a biometric bracelet, AI coaching, a community of like-minded people, and DeFi yield in a single protocol.

Emotional Mining — A New Industry​

Definition​

Emotional Mining is the process of generating economic value from verified data on a person's physical, emotional, and mental wellbeing.

Why This Is an Industry, Not a Feature​

CriterionEmotional Mining
Unique valueFor the first time, emotional wellbeing has a direct financial value
Technology stackBiosensors + ML emotion detection + DeFi + Blockchain oracle
ScalabilityAnyone with a wearable can "mine" — 1B+ potential participants
Economic modelVerifiable wellness data → on-chain proof → yield distribution
AnalogyBitcoin Mining: electricity → computation → value. Emotional Mining: self-care → biometrics → value

WVI as the "Hash Rate" of Emotional Mining​

In Bitcoin Mining, hash rate determines income. In Emotional Mining, WVI (0–100) determines Monthly Yield:

  • WVI is your "hash rate"
  • The bracelet is your "mining rig"
  • Caring for your health is your "energy"
  • Monthly Yield is your "block reward"

Registration: Two Paths​

Path 1: Email-first (primary for APP)​

  1. Registration: Email + password (or Apple ID / Google)
  2. Automatic Safe{Wallet} creation (ERC-4337, hidden from user)
  3. APP onboarding → bracelet connection
  4. Optional: transition to WEB for financial operations

Path 2: WEB3-first (for crypto users)​

  1. Connect existing WEB3 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.)
  2. Sign message (EIP-4361)
  3. Enter email to create Wellex account
  4. Full WEB platform access → optional: download APP

Path 3: Card On-Ramp first (for fiat users)​

  1. Email registration on WEB
  2. Top up via Visa/MC, Apple Pay, Google Pay, SEPA, or PIX → auto-convert to USDC
  3. Auto-deposit into WellexVault
  4. Download APP for bracelet and wellness

All three paths lead to a single account with Safe{Wallet}.

Grace Days — Flexibility Without Punishment​

Problem: WVI = 0 when missing a day — a harsh mechanic that creates stress and churn.

Solution — 3 Grace Days per month:

  • On a Grace Day: WVI = average of the last 7 active days
  • User sees: "📅 Grace Day used (2 of 3 remaining)"
  • During illness/travel: AI Coach automatically offers to activate a Grace Day
  • Grace Days do not accumulate (reset on the 1st of each month)

WVI Floor for stakers:

  • Minimum WVI with active staking = 25 (not 0)
  • With complete non-wear → WVI 25 = 0% yield (not a penalty — simply no bonus)
  • Psychology: "you didn't lose — you just didn't earn today"

Offline Buffer — 48 Hours of Autonomy​

The bracelet stores up to 48 hours of biometric data without phone connection. On reconnection — automatic sync. The user does not lose WVI without connectivity.

Use cases: flights, coverage dead zones, drained phone battery.

AI Yield Engine — Built into the Wallet​

Every ERC-4337 Smart Wallet contains an AI Yield Engine — an autonomous yield optimiser:

  • 800+ strategies from 8 sources (DeFi Lending + RWA + DEX Arbitrage + Prediction Markets + Liquid Staking + Options + Structured Products + Cross-chain)
  • 1M+ parametric combinations optimised with ML methods (gradient boosting + backtesting)
  • Recalculates every 15 minutes, decision latency <500ms
  • User WVI determines allocation: low WVI = conservative portfolio, high = aggressive

The user does not manage strategies manually. Everything is automatic. → 07_AI_SYSTEM.md, 19_WHITEPAPER_v3.md section 9.3

⚠️ Target Monthly Yield 0–20% — not a guarantee. Backtest average (36 months): 0.6–11.4%/month depending on WVI tier. Actual yield depends on market conditions.

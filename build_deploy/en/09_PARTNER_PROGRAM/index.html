09 · WELLEX PARTNER PROGRAM

Version: 5.0 | Date: 05.03.2026 | Status: Canonical Category: Business SSOT for: Compensation plan, ranks, income streams, partner mechanics Dependencies: 04_APP_UX (WVI display), 05_DASHBOARD (WEB partner tools), 11_GO_TO_MARKET (launch strategy), 28_MLM_MARKETING_PLAN (campaign)

⚠️ Partner income depends on personal effort, team size, and market conditions. Target Yield is not a guarantee. See Income Disclosure Statement: wellex.ai/ids

TL;DR​

  • 10 ranks (Explorer → Legend) · 10 levels deep · 8 income streams · $0 entry
  • WVI Performance Bonus: multiplier ×0.80–×1.30 on WEB bonuses · Staking yield 0–20%/month (WVI-based, not a guarantee)
  • Instant Payout: USDC on-chain, <5 minutes — vs. 14–30 days at traditional MLM
  • Partner WVI Lite: floor 40 for active partners — removes "must wear bracelet 24/7" barrier

Purpose & Scope​

This document defines the full Wellex Partner Program: compensation plan, rank system, tools, compliance, and partner activation mechanics. All partner functions are exclusively in the WEB platform — APP is wellness-only. For MLM launch campaign see 28_MLM_MARKETING_PLAN. For GTM strategy see 11_GO_TO_MARKET.

3. Compensation Plan — 8 Income Streams​

3.1 Direct Referral Bonus​

5–8% from each direct referral's subscription. Paid instantly, on-chain.

Important: bonus base = post-swap USDC amount (after on-ramp provider fee). Example: $1,000 via Visa card → on-ramp ~3% fee → $970 USDC. Bonus calculated on post-conversion amount.

3.3 Matching Bonus​

10–25% of direct referrals' team bonuses. Grows with rank: Explorer 10% → Leader 20% → Legend 25%.

3.5 WVI Performance Bonus — the APP↔WEB Bridge​

Partners who wear the bracelet and maintain high WVI in APP receive a multiplier on all WEB bonuses. This is the only intersection point between APP and WEB in the partner program.

3.6 Leadership Pool​

2% of total platform turnover distributed among Leader+ partners proportionally to personal network volume.

3.7 Fast Start Pool​

1% of total turnover — bonus for partners reaching Achiever rank within first 30 days.

3.8 Infinity Bonus (Champion+ and Legend only)​

0.5% from entire network depth without level limits.

4. Global Profit Share (Champion+ becomes co-owners)​

For ranks Champion (9) and Legend (10): ▸ 1% of ALL platform revenue distributed quarterly among Champion+ partners ▸ Distributed proportionally to PV for the quarter ▸ Paid on-chain, publicly verifiable ▸ Makes top leaders psychologically "co-owners of the business" — strongest retention mechanism

8. Partner WVI Lite — Removing the "Bracelet 24/7" Barrier​

MLM leaders 40+ are often not ready to wear a bracelet 24/7. Partner WVI Lite solves this:

▸ Partner WVI Floor = 40 for active partners (≥3 referrals in last 30 days) ▸ Active partner automatically gets minimum WVI 40 — access to all partner bonuses ▸ Those who wear the bracelet and have real WVI 40+ get the multiplier: ×1.15–1.30 ▸ "Must wear bracelet" barrier removed. Bracelet = opportunity, not obligation

9. Micro-Starter Pack — Lowering Entry Barrier​

For emerging markets (SEA, LATAM, CIS) and beginners: ▸ Minimum deposit: $50 (instead of $100) ▸ Micro-staking $50–$499: 0-day lock-up, yield from Day 1 ▸ Partner bonuses from first referral at $50 deposit

11 · GO-TO-MARKET STRATEGY

Version: 5.0 | Date: 05.03.2026 | Status: Canonical Category: Business SSOT for: GTM phases, regional strategy, KOL plan Dependencies: 09_PARTNER_PROGRAM, 23_SALES_PROGRAM, 28_MLM_MARKETING_PLAN

Launch March 2026 · 3 phases · Dubai → CIS → MENA → SEA · KPI · Channel Strategy

⚠️ All yield/income references in marketing materials are Target Yield — not guarantees. Include Income Disclosure Statement link in all partner-facing content.

TL;DR​

  • Dubai as launch hub + CIS parallel launch — March 2026
  • Trust Ladder: Free WVI → Bracelet-only → Full Yield (3 steps before asking for deposits)
  • 100 Founding Ambassadors activated before public launch

Executive Summary​

Wellex enters the market in March 2026 with a focus on Dubai as the launch hub alongside a parallel CIS launch. The strategy spans 36 months, targeting 1M+ users by the end of Phase 3.

Growth channels: organic growth via partner network (MLM) + paid performance marketing + influencers + B2B corporate segment.

Core proposition:

  • Free wellness bracelet with $19/month subscription
  • Monthly Yield 0–20% on deposits
  • Partner programme: 10 ranks, 10 levels of depth

9. Trust Ladder — 3 Steps Before Full Yield Launch​

The key challenge for a new DeFi product: nobody trusts yield without a track record. The Trust Ladder addresses this systematically.

StepPeriodWhat We DoResult
1. Free WVIM1–M2Free registration, manual health data entry (no bracelet), WVI in demo mode10K+ registrations, initial data
2. Bracelet OnlyM2–M3Bracelet + $19/month subscription, real WVI, but yield = demo ("how much you would have earned")5K subscribers, proof of product
3. Full YieldM3+Full yield activation: deposits open, real yield based on WVITVL growth, revenue

Psychology: user sees "could have earned $240 over 2 months" — a powerful trigger for deposit in M3.

10. 100 Founding Ambassadors — Army of Trust Builders​

Programme launches 60 days before Full Yield (Step 3).

Composition of 100 ambassadors:

  • 30 wellness influencers (5K–50K followers)
  • 30 crypto/DeFi bloggers
  • 20 MLM leaders (10K+ networks)
  • 20 biohackers / fitness coaches

Each receives:

  • Bracelet 60 days before public launch
  • Early access to APP
  • Guaranteed Explorer rank (no requirements)
  • Soulbound NFT "Founding Ambassador" + lifetime +1% to yield
  • $500 marketing budget for content

Obligations:

  • 4 posts/month with #EmotionalMining hashtag
  • 1 bracelet video review
  • Participation in weekly Ambassador Chat

Result: 100 × avg 15K reach = 1.5M organic impressions before launch.

11. Wellex 60-Day Challenge — Pre-Launch Viral Campaign​

Concept: 60 days before yield opens — 1,000 users wear the bracelet, accumulate WVI, view demo yield.

Mechanics:

  • Registration: free, bracelet on loan (or buy $99, refund on M3 subscription)
  • Public WVI leaderboard
  • Demo yield visible daily: "Today you would have earned $8.40"
  • Top 100 by WVI at end of 60 days → $10K prize pool (USDC)
  • All participants → automatically first in line for Full Yield

Media narrative: "100 people wore the bracelet for 60 days. Here are their results." → CoinDesk, Decrypt, Forbes Crypto.

13. Diamond Rush — MLM Launch Event​

Conditions: first 500 partners who recruit ≥10 referrals within 30 days after Full Yield → automatic Achiever rank + $500 bonus.

Budget: 500 × $500 = $250K. ROI: each Achiever generates avg $200–500/month PV = $1–2.5M PV/month from 500 leaders.

FOMO mechanic: public counter "Spots remaining: 347 of 500" updates in real time.

14. Leader Migration Package​

Goal: attract 50 top leaders from Herbalife, USANA, PM International, Amway.

Package:

  • Personal onboarding with Account Manager
  • Fast-track to Leader rank (30 days instead of standard 60–90)
  • Exclusive NFT "Proven Leader"
  • +5% bonus to all payouts for first 90 days
  • Direct CEO access on monthly Leader Call

Targeting: LinkedIn + MLM industry conferences + direct outreach via existing partners.

15. Country Captain Programme​

Mechanic: the first partner in any country who recruits 100+ referrals → Country Captain for life.

Country Captain bonuses:

  • +2% of all deposits in their country (lifetime, on-chain)
  • NFT "Country Captain [Country]"
  • Advisory Board seat
  • Quarterly Strategy Call with CEO

Goal: cover 20 countries within 6 months after Full Yield.

23 · SALES PROGRAM

Version: 5.0 | Date: 05.03.2026 | Status: Canonical Category: Business

Sales scripts · Objection handling · Partner onboarding · Training materials Bracelet free with $19/month subscription

Executive Summary​

Wellex sells a $19/month subscription with a free wellness bracelet, rather than selling a device. This dramatically lowers the barrier to entry: $19 instead of $199+.

Key paradigm shift:

ModelOffer
❌ Old"Buy a bracelet for $199"
✅ New"Subscribe for $19/month → get the bracelet free"

Targets: 10,000 subscribers within 3 months · Activation rate >90% · Churn <8%/month

2. Sales Scripts​

2.1 Cold Contact (Telegram/Instagram DM)​

Step 1 — Hook:

"Hi [Name]! I saw your posts about fitness/health — great content! 🔥"

Step 2 — Interest:

"Quick question: if a bracelet tracked your health and paid you up to 20% monthly on your deposit — would you try it?"

Step 3 — Value:

"I've been wearing Wellex for [X] days myself. My WVI is currently [XX], last month's yield was $[XXX]. The bracelet is completely free — subscription is just $19/month."

Step 4 — Call to action:

"Shall I send you a 2-minute video on how it works? Or sign up through my link — 50% off the first month."

  • Agree → link + video
  • Decline → "No worries, save the link in case you change your mind. Good luck! 💪"

2.2 Warm Contact (acquaintance)​

Step 1 — Context:

"[Name], remember when we talked about health/passive income? I found a product that covers both."

Step 2 — Simple explanation:

"Wellex is a bracelet like WHOOP or Oura, but it also pays you. Wear it, live healthy → earn 0 to 20% monthly on your deposit in USDT."

Step 3 — Social proof:

"I've been using it [X] months, earned $[XXX]. Here's my WVI today [show screenshot]. And the bracelet is free — $19/month subscription, that's it."

Step 4 — Risk removal:

"There's a 30-day refund: if you don't like it, you get your $19 back and keep the bracelet. Nothing to lose."

Step 5 — Call to action:

"Shall I sign you up? Takes 2 minutes."

2.3 Partner Opportunity (recruiting)​

Step 1 — Qualification:

"[Name], have you ever thought about an extra income source with zero investment?"

Step 2 — Pitch:

"Wellex has a partner programme: 10 ranks from Explorer to Legend. Explorer — 1 referral, income $20–50/month. Manager (25 referrals) — $500–1,500/month. Legend — $150,000–500,000+/month."

Step 3 — Uniqueness:

"Key differences from traditional MLM:

  • Entry is free — $0
  • No mandatory purchases
  • People actually want this product: free bracelet + passive income from health
  • All payouts in USDC, on blockchain — 100% transparent"

Step 4 — Proof:

"I started [X] months ago, now I'm [rank], earning $[XXX]/month. Here's my dashboard screenshot."

Step 5 — Call to action:

"Want to learn more? There's a 15-minute presentation — I can send it or we can hop on a call."

3. Objection Handling​

Top 15 Objections​

#ObjectionResponse
1"Is this a pyramid/MLM?""The key difference: entry is free, no mandatory purchases, income comes from real subscriptions for a real product. All payouts in USDC on-chain — fully verifiable. 70%+ of users are just users, not partners."
2"$19/month is expensive""$19 is 63 cents a day. For that you get a free bracelet, AI health assistant 24/7, and Monthly Yield up to 20% on deposits. With a $5,000 deposit, yield = $50–85/month. Subscription pays for itself in under 3 months."
3"Where does 5–20% Monthly Yield come from?""Yield is generated through DeFi protocols: Aave, Curve, Pendle. It's real returns from lending and liquidity provision — not thin air and not from new members. The higher your WVI, the larger your share of the pool."
4"I already have an Apple Watch""Apple Watch doesn't pay for health. Wellex is the only product that converts healthy habits into real income. And the bracelet is free — nothing to lose."
5"I don't want to recruit anyone""You don't have to! 80% of users are just users. $19/month subscription → free bracelet → Monthly Yield 5–20% on deposits. The partner programme is optional."
6"No money for a deposit""A deposit isn't required to start. Subscribe for $19/month, get the bracelet, improve your health. When you're ready — deposit any amount from $100 and start earning."
7"I don't understand crypto""You don't need to. The app does everything for you: top up by card, automatic yield, withdraw to card. Like a bank deposit, but more profitable."
8"I need to think about it""Of course! While you think — I'll send a 3-minute video with real user case studies. Remember: 30-day refund if you don't like it."
9"Why haven't I heard of this?""Wellex launches in March 2026. You're finding out among the first — early users always earn more. First partners get a +1% yield bonus for life."
10"Health data is private""All data is encrypted by the TEE chip right on the bracelet. Even Wellex can't see raw data. Full GDPR compliance. You control what you share and with whom."
11"Will the bracelet break?""12–24 month warranty. The bracelet is free — we'll replace it if needed. IP68 waterproof, 14-day battery."
12"Competitors (WHOOP/Oura) are better""WHOOP — $30/month with no yield. Oura — $300 + $6/month with no yield. Wellex — $19/month, free bracelet, plus Monthly Yield 0–20%. The maths is obvious."
13"Too good to be true""I understand the scepticism. That's why: try it free for 30 days. If you don't see results — full refund. Here's my personal experience [show]. Here's yield data [show]."
14"I don't have time""That's exactly why Wellex is perfect: put on the bracelet and forget it. AI does everything for you — recommendations, yield optimisation, even content generation for the partner programme. Lazy Factor is our core principle."

Wellex publishes its Income Disclosure Statement quarterly (not annually — above FTC standard).

IDS MetricDescription

Average incomeArithmetic mean among active partners

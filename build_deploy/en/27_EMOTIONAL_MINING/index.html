2. Definition​

2.1 Formal Definition​

Emotional Mining — the process of generating verifiable economic value from continuous, cryptographically signed data about a person's physical, emotional, and mental wellbeing.

2.2 Process Architecture​

EMOTIONAL MINING
├── INPUT: Health care behavior
│   ├── Emotional wellbeing (HRV, EDA → 11 emotions)
│   ├── Recovery quality (sleep, recovery score)
│   └── Physical activity (steps, workouts)
│
├── PROCESS: Verification and assessment
│   ├── Wellex Band → TEE-signed data
│   ├── ML classification → WVI (0–100)
│   ├── Server-side calculation (anti-cheat)
│   └── On-chain WVI commitment (Oracle)
│
├── OUTPUT: Economic value
│   ├── Monthly Yield 0–20% (from real DeFi)
│   ├── NFT Achievements (Soulbound)
│   ├── Status and reputation in community
│   └── Data value (aggregated, with user consent)
│
└── PROOF: Proof of Wellness
    ├── Cryptographic signature (TEE)
    ├── Cross-validation of 11 metrics
    ├── Biometric fingerprint
    └── Fraud scoring (0–100)

8"How much do I need to invest?""$0 to start partnering. $19/mo subscription. Minimum deposit for yield: $50. Zero mandatory purchases."
9"What if the market crashes?""Dynamic Yield Cap automatically reduces yield if market falls. Insurance Fund (8–12% TVL) covers smart contract and depeg risks. Worst case: you don't lose your deposit, only yield decreases."
10"Better than bank deposits?""Bank: 5% annually (0.4%/mo). Wellex target: 0–20%/mo (at WVI 40+). Plus free AI bracelet, wellness analytics, community. Even at minimum yield — wellness value remains."
11"Why not invest DeFi directly?""DeFi requires expertise, time, monitoring. Wellex AI Yield Engine from 800+ strategies does it automatically. Plus Insurance Fund, Dynamic Yield Cap — protections DeFi doesn't have."
12"Who's behind Wellex?""Alexander Mamasidikov — CEO of CrossFi (L1 blockchain). Documentation: 29+ documents, 250K+ symbols. Trail of Bits audit. Public team, weekly AMA."
13"What if Wellex shuts down?""Funds in DeFi protocols (Aave, Ondo) — not in Wellex accounts. Insurance Fund protects risks. Smart contracts on blockchain — immutable. Multisig 3-of-5."
14"MLM is outdated""Wellex Partner Network is MLM 2.0: digital product (not physical goods), $0 entry, instant on-chain payouts, AI promo generator. Not your grandmother's Amway."
15"Hard to explain to clients""APP is pure wellness. Zero crypto jargon. 'Wear the bracelet → monitor health → earn money'. Partner Academy teaches it in 30 min."
16"No mobile app yet""APP will launch on iOS and Android. MVP → first 90 days. WEB platform already available for financial operations."
17"Where are the licenses?""VARA (UAE) — in process. Legal structure: Cyprus (EU) for Insurance, UAE for operations. Compliance officer from M3."
18"How fast do I get paid?""Instant Payout — on-chain, <5 minutes. Push notification: '💰 You earned $45.20'. Not monthly, not Friday — instantly."
19"I have no audience""Partner Academy teaches from scratch. AI Promo Generator creates content for you. Start with 3 friends — enough for Explorer rank."
20"Sounds too good to be true""Healthy skepticism is good. Here are the facts: Live Yield Dashboard, Proof of Reserves, Chainlink PoR, Trail of Bits audit, Income Disclosure Statement. Verify everything."

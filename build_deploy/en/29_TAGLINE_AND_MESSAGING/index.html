29 · WELLEX — TAGLINE & CORE MESSAGING

Version: 5.0 | Date: 05.03.2026 | Status: Canonical Category: Marketing SSOT for: Brand tagline, core messaging, headline copy Dependencies: 02_PRODUCT_VISION (Emotional Mining), 01_EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY (key metrics)

TL;DR​

  • Primary tagline: "Your Health Earns"
  • Brand line: "The world's first protocol where health generates real income"
  • Everything below — variations by channel, audience, format

1. Message Hierarchy​

1.1 Brand Statement (for all documentation and public presence)​

Wellex — the world's first protocol where caring for your health generates real income.

Used in: website header, About Us, first slide of any presentation, public documents.

1.2 Primary Tagline​

"Your Health Earns"

Short. Memorable on first contact. Works in any context.

Format variations:

  • Full (tagline + explanation): "Your Health Earns. Wellex pays you for being well."
  • Super-short (visuals, badges, stories): "Health = Income"
  • Russian equivalent: «Быть здоровым — выгодно»

1.3 Alternative Tagline (for crypto/DeFi audience)​

"Your WVI is your yield"

For technically literate audience. Appeals to hash rate metaphor.

2. Messaging Architecture (by audience)​

2.1 Mass User (wellness, side hustle)​

ElementText
HeadlineThe bracelet that pays you
SubheadlineWear Wellex → track your health → earn up to 20% monthly on your deposit
CTAStart at $19/mo
ProofBracelet included free. Income from day one. Withdraw anytime.

Elevator pitch (30 sec):

"Wellex is a bracelet that tracks your health and, based on your WVI — a wellness index from 0 to 100 — pays you yield on your USDC deposit. The higher your WVI, the higher your return. Bracelet is free with a $19/mo subscription. No token, no NFT — real DeFi yield."

2.2 Partner / Network Marketing Audience​

ElementText
HeadlineBuild a business on wellness
Subheadline10 ranks · 10 levels · $0 entry · USDC payouts in <5 minutes
CTABecome a Partner
ProofOn-chain payouts. Achiever rank = avg $200–500/mo.

Elevator pitch (30 sec):

"With Wellex you can simultaneously earn on your own health through DeFi yield and build a partner network across 10 levels. Zero entry cost. No forced purchases. USDC payouts — faster than any traditional MLM company."

2.3 B2B (Corporate Wellness)​

ElementText
HeadlineA wellness program employees actually use
SubheadlineBiometric tracking + AI Coach + financial incentive = 60%+ retention
CTARequest a B2B pilot
Proof$99/employee/month. Reduced sick days. Higher engagement.

2.4 Investor / Strategic Partner​

ElementText
HeadlineEmotional Mining: a new industry
SubheadlineThe $6.3T wellness economy + $195B DeFi + AI — the first unified protocol
CTAExplore the documentation: wellex.ai
ProofReal yield from 8 DeFi streams. No token emission. Backtested 36 months.

3. Naming & Vocabulary​

Terms Wellex Introduces to the Market​

TermDefinitionStatus
Emotional MiningThe process of generating income from verified wellbeing dataTrademark pending
WVI (Wellex Vitality Index)Health index 0–100 from three componentsProprietary metric
Yield from WellnessDeFi yield whose size depends on user WVICore narrative
Wellness-to-EarnThe category Wellex creates — healthy lifestyle as income sourceDistinct from Move-to-Earn

What to Avoid in Communications​

❌ Avoid✅ Say instead
"Guaranteed income""Target Yield — up to 20%/mo at WVI 90+"
"Passive income" (unqualified)"DeFi income linked to your WVI"
"Investment""Stablecoin deposit"
"Wellex token""USDC rewards from WellexVault"
"MLM""Partner Network" / "Partner Program"
"Staking" in APPAPP is wellness-only. Staking is WEB-only

4. Core OG Phrases (for content and ads)​

Series A — "Health = Money"​

  1. "Your WVI today is 78. That means 12% monthly. What does your Fitbit mean?"
  2. "For the first time in my life, someone pays me for sleeping."
  3. "You pay for the gym. Wellex pays you for going."
  4. "Wellex: the bracelet that works while you rest."

Series B — "Emotional Mining" (for crypto/tech)​

  1. "Move-to-Earn is dead. Emotional Mining is what comes next."
  2. "WVI is your hash rate. The band is your mining rig. DeFi is the block reward."
  3. "8 DeFi streams. No token. No emission. Real yield."
  4. "Aave + WHOOP + AI Coach. Called Wellex."

Series C — "Social Proof / Income Reveal"​

  1. "60 days on Wellex. WVI: from 52 to 81. Demo yield over the period: $430. Making a deposit now."
  2. "My WVI today is 89 🔥 | wellex.ai"
  3. "Team #WellexChallenge — 8 people, avg WVI 76. 3rd place on the MENA leaderboard."

5. Disclaimers (required in all public materials)​

Standard disclaimer (anywhere yield is mentioned):​

Yield is a target figure and is not a guaranteed return. Actual results depend on DeFi market conditions, user WVI, and WellexVault strategies. Past performance does not guarantee future results. See full terms at wellex.ai

Short disclaimer (for social media / KOL content):​

Target Yield. Not a guarantee. Details: wellex.ai

For partner materials:​

Partner income depends on personal effort, size and activity of your network. Income Disclosure Statement: wellex.ai/ids

32 · "DAY IN THE LIFE" — USER JOURNEY DOCUMENT

Version: 5.0 | Date: 05.03.2026 | Status: Canonical Category: Marketing / Product SSOT for: User journey narratives, onboarding copy, KOL content briefs Dependencies: 04_APP_UX, 03_WELLNESS_SCORE, 06_YIELD_PROTOCOL, 09_PARTNER_PROGRAM

TL;DR​

  • 3 personas — 3 Wellex usage scenarios
  • Each scenario: full day with timestamps, product touchpoints, emotional hooks
  • Used for: KOL briefs, onboarding emails, App Store descriptions, PR materials

Persona 1: Alexandra, 34 — Marketing Manager, Dubai​

"I wanted to earn more. I didn't expect to start sleeping better."

06:45 — Alarm​

Alexandra wakes up. On her wrist — the Wellex Band. Before opening her eyes, she feels a gentle vibration — the morning notification.

She picks up her phone. Opens Wellex.

Good morning, Alexandra ☀️
Sleep: 7h 12min · Recovery: 82 · Streak: 23 days
WVI right now: 76
Your morning forecast: great day ahead 🔥

76 — solid. Yesterday was 71. AI Coach is already analyzing:

"Your HRV is elevated today — 68ms. That's your best reading in 10 days. I recommend capitalizing on this: schedule your most demanding tasks for the first half of the day."

Before, she would simply ignore these patterns. Now — she uses them.

07:15 — Morning Movement and Coffee​

While the coffee brews, Alexandra does 15 minutes of yoga. The bracelet logs the activity. WVI climbs: 76 → 79.

On the APP screen — the team leaderboard. Her team "Dubai Morning Birds" sits 6th out of 340 in the MENA region. 3 WVI points to 5th place.

A small challenge for the day. She loves these.

09:00 — Office​

In the elevator — a quick glance at the APP. One notification from AI Coach:

"You're in the top 15% for consistency this week. Hold WVI above 75 until end of day and you'll activate the Wellex Streak NFT '30 Days'."

Alexandra smiles. 7 days to go.

13:00 — Lunch​

She's at a café with her colleague Dmitry. He notices the bracelet.

— Is that Wellex? I've heard you can earn money with it? — I got 12% on $2,000 this month. $240 in USDC. Landed in my wallet the day before yesterday. — Wait... for what? — For keeping my WVI above 70. The bracelet measures sleep, activity, stress. The higher it is, the higher the yield. — And this actually works? — Here's the transaction. [shows her phone]

Dmitry signs up that same evening.

15:30 — Afternoon Slump​

Most employees hit a concentration dip. AI Coach notices:

"Your stress level has risen (EDA +12%). Perfectly normal for this time of day. Try: 5-minute breathing exercise right now."

Alexandra steps onto the balcony. 5 minutes. Returns with WVI at 77.

Before, she'd reach for a third coffee. Now — she breathes.

18:00 — Heading Home​

On the metro, Alexandra checks the WEB version: her $2,000 deposit, current WVI 78, target yield this month — 13.2%.

In the partner section: +1 referral today — Dmitry registered. She's been credited $5 USDC and 10% of his first subscription.

It's a nice touch, but what really matters to her is the health. The money has become a pleasant side effect.

22:00 — Night​

Alexandra puts the bracelet on for the night. WVI for today: 81 — personal best for the month.

Final notification:

🏆 WVI today: 81
Streak: 23 days
Team rank: 5th in MENA (was 6th!)
Target yield this month: 14.1%
Keep it up, Alexandra. You're in the best shape you've been in 3 months.

She puts her phone down. Falls asleep peacefully.

Persona 2: Michael, 41 — Entrepreneur, Wellex Partner, Almaty​

"I'm building a network. But first — I became the product myself."

06:00 — Before Dawn​

Michael wakes up before everyone else. He is a Wellex partner at Leader rank — 38 active referrals, 4 levels deep in his team.

First thing he sees: the WEB dashboard.

Team: 38 active members
Team PV this month: $18,400
Bonuses credited: $1,847 USDC
Your WVI yesterday: 84 → Personal yield: 18.2%

Two income streams. Both on screen at the same time.

07:30 — Morning Run​

3 km. The bracelet logs: steps, MVPA, Heart Rate Zone 2. WVI grows.

On the way home — a voice call with a new network candidate. Michael says:

"The most common question: 'Does this actually work?' I show my screen. 38 people in the team. $1,847 this month from bonuses alone. Plus 18.2% on my own $5,000 deposit. That's not a promise — that's my screenshot."

10:00 — Wellex Academy​

Michael runs his weekly online webinar for his team. 22 participants.

Today's topic: "How to explain Wellex to someone who's never heard of DeFi."

His 3-step formula:

  1. "Wear the bracelet → your health is measured as a WVI number"
  2. "Deposit USDC → the higher your WVI, the higher your yield"
  3. "Everything is on-chain → a transaction can be verified right now"

His team grows because he teaches — not just recruits.

14:00 — WVI Duel​

One of his referrals challenges Michael to a WVI Duel in the APP. Terms: whoever accumulates more WVI points over 7 days wins the "WVI Duelist" NFT + $25 USDC from the loser.

Michael accepts. Easy — his WVI is 84.

19:00 — End of Day​

MetricValue
WVI today87
Target yield this month18.2%
Team bonus for the day+$89 USDC
New team registrations+1 (Asel from Nur-Sultan)
Streak41 days

Michael closes the laptop. Tomorrow — again.

Persona 3: Anna, 27 — Fitness Trainer, Moscow​

"I was already healthy. Wellex just started paying me for it."

05:45 — Early Workout​

Anna is a fitness trainer. She's up before her clients. Morning cardio session. The band logs:

Sleep: 8h 20min · Recovery: 91
Heart Rate Zone 2: 32 min
Steps: 12,847 for the day (and it's still morning!)
WVI: 94 → Target Yield: 20%/mo

90+ WVI — that's Anna almost every day. The bracelet simply confirms what she's already doing.

11:00 — Between Sessions​

Anna opens WEB. Deposit $800. Target yield 20% → $160/mo in USDC.

Not her effort — her WVI is working for her.

DeFi is simple for her: "I've been into fitness my whole life. Now it's financially rewarding too."

14:00 — Wellex Community​

In APP — group "Moscow Biohackers". 47 members. Anna is in the top 3 by WVI in the group.

Her post today:

"WVI 94 ✨ | 23km steps over 2 days | Sleep score 91 | Wellex has already credited $147 on my deposit this month 💸 | What I love about Wellex — my lifestyle literally earns money now. #EmotionalMining wellex.ai"

127 likes. 14 reposts. 3 people asked for her referral link.

23:00 — Day's End​

WVI today: 94
Personal record: 96 (a week ago)
Target yield this month: 20%
Monthly progress: $147 of $160 credited
Streak: 67 days 🔥

Anna smiles. She's just living the way she always has. Wellex just made it profitable.

How to Use This Document​

For KOL Briefs​

"Show your real day with Wellex. Morning, WVI check, AI Coach, friends/colleagues — their reaction, end-of-day recap. Not a script — your life. Hashtag #EmotionalMining. Link wellex.ai"

For Onboarding Email (Day 3)​

"How's your WVI going? Here's how Alexandra from Dubai spent her first month..." (link to Persona 1)

For App Store / Google Play Description​

Use micro-moments from Persona 1:

"Wake up with your WVI report. Get real-time AI recommendations. Earn up to 20%/mo on your deposit — just by living healthier."

For PR (Forbes, CoinDesk)​

Use Persona 2 (Michael) — a partner story with concrete numbers, dual income stream.

33 · APP STORE STRATEGY

Version: 5.0 | Date: 05.03.2026 | Status: Canonical Category: Growth / Product SSOT for: ASO, App Store / Google Play listing, review strategy, organic mobile acquisition Dependencies: 04_APP_UX, 11_GO_TO_MARKET, 29_TAGLINE_AND_MESSAGING

TL;DR​

  • ASO is the key channel for long-term organic growth (Year 2+, target 30% new users from App Store)
  • Two separate listings: iOS App Store + Google Play Store
  • Strategy based on wellness + passive income keywords — not crypto/DeFi (compliance)
  • Review strategy: 4.7+ rating from the first 90 days

1. Context and Positioning​

Why App Store Is a Separate Strategy​

The App Store and Google Play are not just distribution platforms. They are search engines with 650M+ queries/day (Apple Search Ads data, 2025). Correct optimization = constant organic traffic without ad spend.

Wellex's specifics in the App Store:

⚠️ Critical: Apple and Google have strict rules for financial and crypto apps. The Wellex APP is a wellness application, not a financial one. Staking, deposits, yield — all of this is WEB-only. This is not just a product decision — it is a compliance strategy for the App Store.

What NOT to do in App Store listingWhy
Mention DeFi, yield, stakingApple/Google financial rules (Guideline 3.1.1)
Promise specific % returnsViolation of financial regulations
Mention crypto, blockchain in main descriptionTriggers additional Apple review
Call the APP a walletApp Store Guideline 3.1.5 (crypto wallet rules)
What to doResult
Position as wellness + AI coachingFast moderation
"Earn while staying healthy" — no specific %Clean compliance
Biometric tracking + community — core narrativeTop Health & Fitness category
"Unlock rewards" instead of "earn DeFi yield"Passes review

5. Review Strategy​

5.1 Target: 4.7+ Rating from Day 1​

Why this matters: App Store algorithm weights rating heavily in search ranking. 4.7+ = top-5% of apps.

5.2 In-App Review Prompts​

Trigger review request ONLY at high-satisfaction moments:

TriggerMomentExpected sentiment
Streak milestone7 daysHigh satisfaction
WVI improvement+10 points in 2 weeksPositive emotion
First yield creditedMonth 1Financial win
Team challenge wonAny timeSocial win
AI Coach follow-throughAfter 3 recommendations acted onTrust built

Prompt copy:

"Your WVI improved 12 points this week. How's your Wellex experience?" [Rate / Not now]

Rule: Never prompt within 14 days of last prompt.

7. Launch Checklist​

Pre-launch (2 weeks before):

  • App icon finalized and A/B ready
  • All 5 screenshots localized (EN + RU)
  • App preview video produced
  • Description copy reviewed for compliance (no DeFi mentions)
  • Keywords filed in iOS Keyword field
  • Privacy policy URL live at wellex.ai/privacy

Launch day:

  • Submit for App Store review (allow 48–72h)
  • Submit for Google Play review (allow 24–48h)
  • Announce launch on all channels
  • Alert beta users to leave reviews

Post-launch week 1:

  • Monitor review rating daily
  • Respond to all reviews within 24h
  • Check keyword ranking (AppFollow / Sensor Tower)
  • Analyze install source breakdown

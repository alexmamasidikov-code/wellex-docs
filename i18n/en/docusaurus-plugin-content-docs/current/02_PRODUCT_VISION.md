## Emotional Mining — A New Industry

### Definition

**Emotional Mining** is the process of generating economic value from verified data on a person's physical, emotional, and mental wellbeing.

### Why This Is an Industry, Not a Feature

| Criterion | Emotional Mining |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Unique value** | For the first time, emotional wellbeing has a direct financial value |
| **Technology stack** | Biosensors + ML emotion detection + DeFi + Blockchain oracle |
| **Scalability** | Anyone with a wearable can "mine" — 1B+ potential participants |
| **Economic model** | Verifiable wellness data → on-chain proof → yield distribution |
| **Analogy** | Bitcoin Mining: electricity → computation → value. Emotional Mining: self-care → biometrics → value |

### Evolution of Mining

| Generation | Model | Resource | Limitation |
|-----------|-------|----------|------------|
| **Bitcoin Mining** | Proof of Work | Electricity + GPU | Energy consumption, centralisation |
| **Emotional Mining (Wellex)** | Proof of Wellness | Emotions + Sleep + Activity | Sustainable model — yield from real DeFi |

### WVI as the "Hash Rate" of Emotional Mining

In Bitcoin Mining, hash rate determines income. In Emotional Mining, **WVI (0–100)** determines **Monthly Yield**:

- WVI is your "hash rate"
- The bracelet is your "mining rig"
- Caring for your health is your "energy"
- **Monthly Yield** is your "block reward"

## Grace Days — Flexibility Without Punishment

**Problem:** WVI = 0 when missing a day — a harsh mechanic that creates stress and churn.

**Solution — 3 Grace Days per month:**
- On a Grace Day: WVI = average of the last 7 active days
- User sees: "📅 Grace Day used (2 of 3 remaining)"
- During illness/travel: AI Coach automatically offers to activate a Grace Day
- Grace Days do not accumulate (reset on the 1st of each month)

**WVI Floor for stakers:**
- Minimum WVI with active staking = 25 (not 0)
- With complete non-wear → WVI 25 = 0% yield (not a penalty — simply no bonus)
- Psychology: *"you didn't lose — you just didn't earn today"*

## 3. Compensation Plan — 8 Income Streams

### 3.1 Direct Referral Bonus
5–8% from each direct referral's subscription.
Paid instantly, on-chain.

**Important: bonus base = post-swap USDC amount** (after on-ramp provider fee).
Example: $1,000 via Visa card → on-ramp ~3% fee → $970 USDC. Bonus calculated on post-conversion amount.

### 3.2 Team Bonus (10 levels deep)

| Level | % of PV | Available from rank |
|:-----:|:-------:|:-------------------:|
| 1 | 10.0% | Explorer 🧭 |
| 2 | 7.0% | Builder 🔧 |
| 3 | 5.0% | Achiever 🏅 |
| 4 | 4.0% | Manager 🔷 |
| 5 | 3.0% | Director 💎 |
| 6 | 2.0% | Leader 🛡️ |
| 7 | 1.5% | Ambassador 🌐 |
| 8 | 1.0% | Master 👁️ |
| 9 | 0.75% | Champion 👑 |
| 10 | 0.5% | Legend 🔱 |

**Dynamic Compression:** if a partner at an intermediate level is inactive, the bonus "compresses" upward — the upline partner's income is not affected by inactive participants.

### 3.3 Matching Bonus
10–25% of direct referrals' team bonuses. Grows with rank: Explorer 10% → Leader 20% → Legend 25%.

### 3.4 Rank Achievement Bonus (one-time)

| Rank | Bonus |
|------|:-----:|
| Explorer 🧭 | $25 |
| Builder 🔧 | $100 |
| Achiever 🏅 | $500 |
| Manager 🔷 | $2,000 |
| Director 💎 | $5,000 |
| Leader 🛡️ | $15,000 |
| Ambassador 🌐 | $30,000 |
| Master 👁️ | $75,000 |
| Champion 👑 | $150,000 |
| Legend 🔱 | $500,000 |

### 3.5 WVI Performance Bonus — the APP↔WEB Bridge

Partners who wear the bracelet and maintain high WVI in APP receive a multiplier on all WEB bonuses. This is the **only intersection point between APP and WEB** in the partner program.

| Partner WVI (from APP) | Multiplier on WEB bonuses |
|:----------------------:|:-------------------------:|
| 0–40 | ×0.80 (reduction) |
| 41–60 | ×1.00 (base) |
| 61–80 | ×1.15 (+15%) |
| 81–100 | ×1.30 (+30%) |

### 3.6 Leadership Pool
2% of total platform turnover distributed among Leader+ partners proportionally to personal network volume.

### 3.7 Fast Start Pool
1% of total turnover — bonus for partners reaching Achiever rank within first 30 days.

### 3.8 Infinity Bonus (Champion+ and Legend only)
0.5% from entire network depth without level limits.

## 8. Partner WVI Lite — Removing the "Bracelet 24/7" Barrier

MLM leaders 40+ are often not ready to wear a bracelet 24/7. Partner WVI Lite solves this:

▸ **Partner WVI Floor = 40** for active partners (≥3 referrals in last 30 days)
▸ Active partner automatically gets minimum WVI 40 — access to all partner bonuses
▸ Those who wear the bracelet and have real WVI 40+ get the multiplier: ×1.15–1.30
▸ "Must wear bracelet" barrier removed. Bracelet = opportunity, not obligation

## 10. Partner Toolkit (WEB only)

| Tool | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Referral link | Personal link + QR code + deep link |
| Network structure | Visual tree up to 10 levels deep. Clicking on a partner opens a mandatory popup with 4 fields (see below) |

| Analytics | Conversion, activity, progress to next rank |

### Partner Profile Popup — Mandatory Fields on Click

Clicking any partner in the network tree must display the following fields:

| # | Field | Data |
|---|-------|------|
| 1 | **Personal staking amount** | $ in WellexVault (on-chain real-time) |
| 2 | **Total network volume** | PV of the partner's entire network for 30 days |
| 3 | **Number of active partners** | Partners with staking ≥$50 or ≥1 referral in 30 days |
| 4 | **Gap to next rank** | Missing PV needed for rank advancement |

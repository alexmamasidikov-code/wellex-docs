## 9. Trust Ladder — 3 Steps Before Full Yield Launch

The key challenge for a new DeFi product: nobody trusts yield without a track record. The Trust Ladder addresses this systematically.

| Step | Period | What We Do | Result |
|------|--------|-----------|--------|
| **1. Free WVI** | M1–M2 | Free registration, manual health data entry (no bracelet), WVI in demo mode | 10K+ registrations, initial data |
| **2. Bracelet Only** | M2–M3 | Bracelet + $19/month subscription, real WVI, but yield = demo ("how much you would have earned") | 5K subscribers, proof of product |
| **3. Full Yield** | M3+ | Full yield activation: deposits open, real yield based on WVI | TVL growth, revenue |

**Psychology:** user sees "could have earned $240 over 2 months" — a powerful trigger for deposit in M3.

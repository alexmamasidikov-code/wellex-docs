## Architecture of the Future

Wellex is not just a wellness bracelet. It is a **protocol for monetizing human physical and emotional capital**. Six mega-features transform the product into an ecosystem — each unlocking a new TAM.

```
2026 (Y1): Band + APP + WEB + AI Yield Engine → Core Product
```

## 1. Wellex Card 💳

**Concept:** Visa/Mastercard debit card linked to WellexVault. Spend yield directly — no withdrawal needed.

**Mechanics:**
- Card balance = yield account in WellexVault
- Each transaction: real-time debit from yield (USDC → USD/EUR/AED)
- 1–3% cashback on wellness purchases (pharmacy, fitness, healthy food) — paid in Wellex Points
- Physical card + Apple Pay / Google Pay

**WVI perks:**
- WVI 70+: free card maintenance (0%)
- WVI 90+: enhanced 3% cashback + travel insurance

**Business model:**
- Interchange fee: 1.5–2% per transaction (standard Visa/MC for fintech)
- Currency conversion: 0.5% spread
- Premium tier (WVI 90+): $9.99/mo for premium card

**TAM:** $10T+ global card spend. **Revenue Y3 (est.):** $3–5M
**Dependencies:** E-money license (EU) or BIN-sponsor partnership. Sumsub KYC.
**Timeline:** Q2 2027

## 2. Wellex Health Passport 🏥

**Concept:** Portable, verified medical profile based on WVI data. Integration with insurance companies — discounts on policies for good health.

**Mechanics:**
- 12+ months of WVI data = verified wellness score
- Health Passport NFT: on-chain verified document (anonymized)
- Integration with partner insurers (AXA, Allianz, DAO InsurACE): present Passport → 5–15% policy discount
- Partner medical facilities: Passport → priority booking, discounts

**Monetization:**
- Referral fee from insurers: $20–50 per converted user
- Data (opt-in): anonymized aggregated insights sold to insurers and pharma
- Health Passport NFT mint fee: $29 (one-time)

**TAM:** $6.3T global wellness + $1.5T health insurance market
**Revenue Y3 (est.):** $2–4M
**Timeline:** Q3 2027

## 3. Emotional Mining Marketplace 🎯

**Concept:** The world's first marketplace where users monetize their anonymized biometric data directly. 50/50 revenue share.

**Mechanics:**
- User decides: opt-in to data sales (aggregated and anonymized only)
- Buyers: pharmaceutical companies, insurers, wellness brands, research institutes
- Minimum dataset for sale: 90+ days of data
- Price per dataset: $5–50/mo depending on volume and WVI tier
- 50% — to the user (additional income), 50% — to the platform

**Protection:**
- Zero-knowledge proof: buyer sees only aggregated patterns
- User sees: who bought, for how much, which data
- Consent revocation: one button, immediate deletion

**TAM:** $3B global health data market, growing 25%/year
**Revenue Y3 (est.):** $5–10M (at 100K opt-in users)
**Dependencies:** GDPR Art. 9 compliance. Legal opinions in each jurisdiction. Secure MPC (multi-party computation) for privacy.
**Timeline:** Q1 2028

**Concept:** White-label wellness program for corporations. $29/employee/mo — each gets a bracelet + WVI + AI Coach + corporate challenges.

**Product:**

- Team challenges: "Sales vs Engineering" by WVI
- Corporate reports: wellness trends, burnout risk, productivity correlation
- Customization: white label + corporate branding

**Monetization:**
- $29/employee/mo (vs $19 B2C — premium for corporate features)

**TAM:** $60B global corporate wellness market. 60% of Fortune 500 companies have wellness programs.
**Target segment:** 50–5,000 employees. First 10 clients: tech companies, insurers.
**Revenue Y3 (est.):** $3–8M (5–20 corporate clients × $100–$500K/year)
**Dependencies:** B2B sales team (CMO + 2 Account Executives). Legal: corporate contracts, SLA.
**Timeline:** Q4 2026 (pilot), Q2 2027 (full launch)

## 6. Wellex DAO 🗳️

**Concept:** Handing protocol governance to the community. Legend-rank partners and long-term stakers receive governance rights.

**Mechanics:**
- Governance Points (GP) — non-fungible, non-transferable — earned through:
  - Staking duration (1 GP/mo/year)
  - WVI streaks (bonus GP for streaks of 100+, 200+, 365+ days)
  - Partner program rank (Legend = maximum weight)
- Votes: new strategies for AI Yield Engine, WVI table changes, Treasury allocation

**Treasury:**
- 5% of platform revenue → DAO Treasury (after Y2)
- Usage: ecosystem grants, new market expansions, research grants

**Why no fungible token:**
- No speculation, no unsustainable tokenomics
- Governance Points cannot be purchased — only earned through activity
- Regulatorily clean mechanism (not a security, not a commodity)

**Timeline:** Q2 2029

## Executive Summary

Wellex sells a **$19/month subscription with a free wellness bracelet**, rather than selling a device. This dramatically lowers the barrier to entry: $19 instead of $199+.

## 9. Income Disclosure Statement (IDS)

> ⚠️ **Mandatory:** an IDS link must be present in all promo materials, presentations, and partner programme scripts.

**Wellex publishes its Income Disclosure Statement quarterly** (not annually — above FTC standard).

| IDS Metric | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| % active partners | Share of partners with ≥1 payout in the period |
| Median income | 50th percentile among active partners |
| Average income | Arithmetic mean among active partners |
| Maximum income | Top-1 partner for the period |
| % with no income | Share of registered partners with $0 payouts |
| Distribution by rank | Income by each of the 10 ranks |

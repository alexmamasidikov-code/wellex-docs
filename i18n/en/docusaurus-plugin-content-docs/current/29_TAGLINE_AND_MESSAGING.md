*Short. Memorable on first contact. Works in any context.*

## 3. Naming & Vocabulary

### Terms Wellex Introduces to the Market

| Term | Definition | Status |
|------|------------|--------|
| **Emotional Mining** | The process of generating income from verified wellbeing data | Trademark pending |
| **WVI (Wellex Vitality Index)** | Health index 0–100 from three components | Proprietary metric |
| **Yield from Wellness** | DeFi yield whose size depends on user WVI | Core narrative |
| **Wellness-to-Earn** | The category Wellex creates — healthy lifestyle as income source | Distinct from Move-to-Earn |

### What to Avoid in Communications

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Say instead |
|----------|---------------|
| "Guaranteed income" | "Target Yield — up to 20%/mo at WVI 90+" |
| "Passive income" (unqualified) | "DeFi income linked to your WVI" |
| "Investment" | "Stablecoin deposit" |
| "Wellex token" | "USDC rewards from WellexVault" |
| "MLM" | "Partner Network" / "Partner Program" |
| "Staking" in APP | APP is wellness-only. Staking is WEB-only |

## 1. Context and Positioning

### Why App Store Is a Separate Strategy

The App Store and Google Play are not just distribution platforms. They are search engines with 650M+ queries/day (Apple Search Ads data, 2025). Correct optimization = constant organic traffic without ad spend.

**Wellex's specifics in the App Store:**

⚠️ **Critical:** Apple and Google have strict rules for financial and crypto apps. The Wellex APP is a **wellness application**, not a financial one. Staking, deposits, yield — all of this is WEB-only. This is not just a product decision — it is a compliance strategy for the App Store.

| What NOT to do in App Store listing | Why |
|------------------------------------|-----|
| Mention DeFi, yield, staking | Apple/Google financial rules (Guideline 3.1.1) |
| Promise specific % returns | Violation of financial regulations |
| Mention crypto, blockchain in main description | Triggers additional Apple review |
| Call the APP a wallet | App Store Guideline 3.1.5 (crypto wallet rules) |

| What to do | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Position as wellness + AI coaching | Fast moderation |
| "Earn while staying healthy" — no specific % | Clean compliance |
| Biometric tracking + community — core narrative | Top Health & Fitness category |
| "Unlock rewards" instead of "earn DeFi yield" | Passes review |

## 5. Review Strategy

### 5.1 Target: 4.7+ Rating from Day 1

**Why this matters:** App Store algorithm weights rating heavily in search ranking. 4.7+ = top-5% of apps.

### 5.2 In-App Review Prompts

Trigger review request ONLY at high-satisfaction moments:

| Trigger | Moment | Expected sentiment |
|---------|--------|-------------------|
| Streak milestone | 7 days | High satisfaction |
| WVI improvement | +10 points in 2 weeks | Positive emotion |
| First yield credited | Month 1 | Financial win |
| Team challenge won | Any time | Social win |
| AI Coach follow-through | After 3 recommendations acted on | Trust built |

**Prompt copy:**
> "Your WVI improved 12 points this week. How's your Wellex experience?" [Rate / Not now]

**Rule:** Never prompt within 14 days of last prompt.

### 5.3 Review Response Policy

| Review type | Response time | Tone |
|-------------|:------------:|------|
| 5-star | 48h | Thank + share tip |
| 4-star | 24h | Thank + address concern |
| 3-star | 24h | Thank + collect feedback |
| 1–2 star | 12h | Empathize + offer support + escalate to team |

**Response template (1-star):**
> "We're sorry to hear your experience wasn't what we hoped. Our team is here to help — please reach out at support@wellex.ai with your account details and we'll make this right."
